Our Story

At Il Gambero Rosso we’re all about delicious Italian food with a twist to surprise and delight all tastes
because good food does more than fill you up, it brings people together.
Since first opening our doors and firing up our signature pizza ovens in Chiswick over 20 years ago
we’ve opened over 130 restaurants across the UK & Ireland, each individually designed
with touches unique to the local area and perfect for all occasions.

Our RESTAURANTS are the perfect place to get together. We’ve thought of everything
from tasty cocktails to 18 inch Rustica Pizzas.

All you need to do is sit back, relax and we’ll take care of the rest.



Why choose us


Since 1931, we’ve come to realize that things have really changed. But a taste of the past can be fundamental when building tradition with your family and friends. There’s something that can be said about being served up a heap of pasta, topped in a sauce that’s been simmered the same way for over almost 90 years. A lot of Italian restaurants have modernized and strayed from an authentic experience, but we pride ourselves in giving you a traditional meal that will bring back memories and help you build new ones.
